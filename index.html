General Health

Not feeling well or just need an annual exam? We do it all! We also perform sports physicals and referrals for injuries. Whether you're sick or only need some routine maintenance, we're here for you!

The Practice

Our Health Mission

Our experienced medical professionals put your healing needs first. We are proud to provide a high quality level of customer service, medical experience, and commitment to health and wellness to all our patients. Our goal is to make you feel better as quickly as possible.

Experience and Professionalism

With years of experience, our medical team will assess you and create a custom recovery plan that's right for you. We understand the importance of educating you on the most effective ways to take care of your body, so that you can heal quickly.

Physicians Who Care

Not only will our doctors treat your existing conditions, we also work to prevent pain and illness from occurring. We strive to help you improve your quality of life, achieve your wellness goals, and heal your body to live your best life possible.

Our Doctors

Muhammad Shahzad, MD, FACP
Internal Medicine

Dr. Shahzad attended Rawalpinidi Medical University in Rawalpindi, Punjab, Pakistani. He completed his residency at Ravenswood Hospital in Northside Chicago. Dr. Shahzad has been practicing medicine for more than 20 years and in addition to Internal Medicine he also specializes in Hospital Medicine.

Dr. Shahzad is affiliated with numerous Hospitals, Rehabs and Nursing Homes, Home Health Agencies. He is able to carry on the continuity of care when it's needed from the clinic, to the hospital, to rehab, and back to home.

Dr. Shahzad believes in always learning and always teaching. He not only takes great pride in the medical students he mentors but also believes in educating every patient to help them better understand their own health and to be actively involved in their medical care.

Krishdeep Khosla, MD
Internal Medicine

Dr. Khosla attended Government Medical College in India. He completed his resident at Chicago Medical School in North Chicago. Dr. Khosla's medical background includes over 20 years of experience in chronic and acute care.

Dr. Khosla is affiliated with numerous Hospitals and Rehab and Nursing homes in Cook, Dupage, and Lake county.

Dr. Khosla's philosophy of care is "providing care with dignity and respect".

Amina Baloch, APN
Adult-Gero NP

Amina graduated Summa Cum Laude from Loyola Neihoff School of Nursing, where she also received the Julia Lane Silver Key Award. She then went on to obtain a Masters in Nursing from Maryland University in St. Louis, Missouri, specialized in Adult-Gero Primary Care.
